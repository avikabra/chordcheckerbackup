External Resources

The following are links to free websites, apps, and other features that will supplement your journey as a guitarist:

Music Resources: (all free to use)

Ultimate Guitar - a site which has a strong community and numerous easy-to-play tabs (my personal favorite)

Songsterr - an extremely user-friendly website with loads of tabs ranging in difficulty

YouTube! - surprisingly, YouTube is one of the best methods to find the scores or tabs of popular, mainstream songs (look in video descriptions)

Learning Applications:

Yousician - an application that effectively teaches you how to play a range of instruments

Guitar Tricks - the best bet if you want an app with a comprehensive bank of lessons and songs coupled with a user-friendly platform

Mobile Individual Note Tuners: (all free to use)

Guitar Tuna - my personal favorite, it has tuning ranging across different instruments and keys and a nice aesthetic

Pano Tuner - with high ratings in both the Android and iOS stores, this tuner is a quick and convenient tuner when you're on your phone
